About Our Fencing

Our natural willow fencing is crafted from *Salix viminalis*, a fast-growing, eco-friendly material known for its durability and aesthetic charm.

Benefits include:

  • Eco-friendly and biodegradable
  • Strong and flexible for lasting fences
  • Perfect for privacy, windbreaks, and garden aesthetics
  • Supports biodiversity and sustainable practices
